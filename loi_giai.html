Giải phương trình \(\frac{\log{\left (4 x \right )}}{\log{\left (2 \right )}} - \frac{\log{\left (2 \right )}}{\log{\left (\frac{x}{2} \right )}} = 3\)
\(\frac{\log{\left (4 x \right )}}{\log{\left (2 \right )}} - \frac{\log{\left (2 \right )}}{\log{\left (\frac{x}{2} \right )}} = 3\)
\(\Leftrightarrow -3 + \frac{\log{\left (4 x \right )}}{\log{\left (2 \right )}} - \frac{\log{\left (2 \right )}}{\log{\left (\frac{x}{2} \right )}} = 0\)
\(\Leftrightarrow \frac{\left(- \log{\left (x \right )} + \log{\left (4 \right )}\right) \log{\left (x \right )}}{\left(- \log{\left (x \right )} + \log{\left (2 \right )}\right) \log{\left (2 \right )}} = 0\)
\(\Leftrightarrow \frac{\left(- \log{\left (x \right )} + 2 \log{\left (2 \right )}\right) \log{\left (x \right )}}{\left(- \log{\left (x \right )} + \log{\left (2 \right )}\right) \log{\left (2 \right )}} = 0\)
\(\Leftrightarrow x=\begin{cases}1 \\ 4 \\ \end{cases}\)
